I've been working as a full-time professional game developer for the past three years. I have experience working on large AAA and small scale projects as a Technical Designer and within Tools.

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 - 2023

  • Lead the creation of a brand-new CCTV camera mechanic from prototype to ship in the level Trojan Horse, which is now utilized in future installments and by other studios.

  • Implemented running trains in a London environment during gameplay for the level Trojan Horse.
